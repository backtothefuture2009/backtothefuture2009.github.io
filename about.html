Why We Built Launchpad Teens

Launchpad Teens was born out of a shared desire to bridge the gap between students' potential and their future careers. We believe that with the right guidance and support, every teen can launch into a future where they are not only employable but thriving in what they love. Whether it’s helping students explore tech, medicine, design, or entrepreneurship—or giving innovators a space to pitch ideas—we are building the foundation for tomorrow's changemakers.
